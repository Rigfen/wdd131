Why Paraglide?

Experience freedom, adrenaline, and breathtaking views.Paragliding is a recreational, foot-launched adventure air sport that allows individuals to experience flight in its purest form. It involves piloting a lightweight, non-motorized, free-flying glider aircraft that has no rigid structure, relying instead on a flexible, wing-shaped fabric canopy
